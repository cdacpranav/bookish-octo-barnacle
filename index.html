⚡ ACTION REQUIRED — Book Bhasma Aarti Separately!

Bhasma Aarti is NOT included in the package. This is the #1 reason people visit Ujjain — book it yourself NOW.

  • Book at shrimahakaleshwar.com or mahakaleshwar.nic.in
  • Only ~150 online slots per day — sells out days in advance
  • Aarti happens daily at 4:00 AM — reach temple by 3:00 AM
  • Need Aadhaar number for registration. Print confirmation.
  • Best day: Day 1 (June 25) — arrive 6:25 AM, but aarti is at 4 AM, so you'd miss it. Ask operator to arrange Day 2 early start OR arrive a day early.
  • Alternative: Ask operator if Day 1 can start with Bhasma Aarti if you reach early, or request Day 2 pre-dawn slot.
🌡️

June Weather — Extreme Heat (40–44°C)

Late June is pre-monsoon in MP. Temperatures hit 40–44°C daytime. Monsoon expected to arrive around June 17–20, so by June 25 there may be brief heavy showers too.

  • Daytime: 38–44°C (feels like 46°C with humidity)
  • Mandatory: ORS packets, sunscreen SPF 50+, 2L water bottle, cap/umbrella
  • Rest from 1 PM–4 PM — hotel AC time is NON-NEGOTIABLE
  • Best temple hours: 6–11 AM and 4–7 PM
  • Possible pre-monsoon showers: carry compact umbrella

Optimization & Survival

🔥 Bhasma Aarti Guide

  • Book at shrimahakaleshwar.com — only ~150 slots/day
  • Need Aadhaar for registration. 1 person can book for group.
  • Reach temple by 3:00 AM. Gate opens early for ticket holders.
  • Wear simple cotton dhoti/kurta — no jeans/fancy clothes.
  • No phones, cameras, bags inside inner sanctum.
  • Ritual takes ~2 hours. Standing entire time. Carry patience.

🌡️ Heat Survival (44°C)

  • Drink 4–5 liters water per day — not optional!
  • ORS every 3–4 hours. Prevent dehydration before it starts.
  • NEVER skip 1–4 PM rest. Heat stroke is real and dangerous.
  • Cotton, light-colored, loose clothing. Synthetic = suffering.
  • Sunscreen every 3 hours, even inside auto-rickshaws.
  • Drink nimbu-pani or buttermilk at every opportunity.
  • Dizzy/nauseous? STOP. Shade. Pour water on head & neck.

🚫 Common Mistakes

  • Don't trust temple touts at gates. Use official counters only.
  • Don't pay >₹500 for "special pooja" — negotiate or use official.
  • Don't plan anything between 12–4 PM in June. Just don't.
  • Don't forget to book Bhasma Aarti — walk-in is chaos.
  • Don't carry valuables to temples — leave in hotel safe.
  • Don't drink tap water — bottled/filtered only.
  • Don't eat heavy lunch in June — keep it light, heavier dinner.
